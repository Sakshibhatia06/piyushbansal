About Us

Piyush Bansal is the co-founder of Lykans Realty, a groundbreaking force in Dubai's real estate market. With an educational foundation in architecture and an entrepreneurial spirit, he has seamlessly blended creativity, strategy, and innovation to transform dreams into
reality.
In just 9 months, under Piyush's leadership, Lykans Realty has achieved unparalleled success. The company has become the No. 1 seller of DAMAC properties, a remarkable achievement that showcases Piyush’s dedication to excellence and client satisfaction. Recognized by DAMAC Developers and featured in the prestigious Khaleej Times as one of Dubai’s top 50 fastest-growing companies, Lykans Realty is a testament to his relentless drive and strategic vision.

  • Co-founder of Lykans Realty, a trailblazer in Dubai’s real estate market, blending architecture, strategy, and innovation to achieve excellence.
  • Diversifies his ventures as owner and shareholder of Lykans Finance and Marker Finance, showcasing leadership in both real estate and financial services.
  • A passionate mentor, inspiring aspiring entrepreneurs and professionals with his insights and success journey.
